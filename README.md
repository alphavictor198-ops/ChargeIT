# GatiCharge: Intelligent EV Mobility & Safety Ecosystem 🚗⚡🧠

**"Where Vehicle Energy Meets Human Safety."**

GatiCharge is a premium, high-fidelity smart mobility platform designed to accelerate EV adoption by solving the dual challenges of range anxiety and driver fatigue. It features a synchronized mobile and web ecosystem powered by proprietary physics and biological monitoring engines.

---

## 🏗️ Project Overview
The GatiCharge platform integrates real-time station data, physics-based route optimization, and human-state monitoring to provide a seamless charging and driving experience.

### 🔴 The Problem
*   **Range Anxiety**: Inaccurate range predictions lead to charging uncertainty.
*   **Driver Fatigue**: Long EV journeys often lead to fatigue, a major cause of highway accidents.
*   **Infrastructure Fragmentation**: Real-time availability and slot booking are often unreliable or non-integrated.

### 🟢 The Solution (GatiCharge)
*   **Range Precision**: Physics-based energy modeling (aero drag, rolling resistance, HVAC).
*   **Human Safety**: Real-time Biological State Monitoring (HSS) and Synchronized Stop Engine.
*   **Hardware Handshake**: OCPP-integrated slot booking and live station occupancy flux.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🧠 Core Intelligence Engines
*   **Physics Consumption Model**: Real-time energy prediction accounting for speed, temperature, and load.
*   **A* Route Optimizer**: Minimizes charging stops while guaranteeing a safe SoC arrival.
*   **Human State Score (HSS)**: Monitors driver alertness and "jerk" events (0.85g sensitivity) using phone sensors.
*   **Synchronized Stop Engine**: Aligns vehicle charging stops with driver fatigue/rest requirements.

### 📱 Mobile Experience (React Native/Expo)
*   **High-Fidelity HUD**: Distraction-free active trip interface with neon aesthetics.
*   **Biological Alerts**: Visual and haptic feedback for fatigue and sudden jerk detection.
*   **Passenger-Aware Profiles**: Adapts routing for infants, elderly, pets, and children.
*   **Universal Map**: Live discovery of 312 highway hubs with real-time slot occupancy.

### 💻 Web Intelligence Portal (Next.js)
*   **Platform Parity**: Full synchronization of data, logic, and visual branding with the mobile app.
*   **Vehicle Handshake**: One-click instant vehicle connection simulation.
*   **Enterprise Suite**: CPO Admin Panel, Fleet Command, and AI Demand Forecasting.
*   **Optimized Planner**: Large-screen interactive trip planning with confidence intervals.

### 🛡️ Enterprise Suite
*   **CPO Admin**: Manage hardware health, pricing, and OCPP logs.
*   **Fleet Command**: Centralized telemetry and safety monitoring for multiple units.
*   **AI Demand Forecast**: Predictive heatmaps for infrastructure expansion.
